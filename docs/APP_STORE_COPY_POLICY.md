# App Store Copy Policy

## Purpose
Prevent marketing-copy drift from inflating product authority beyond evidence-bound scope.

## Forbidden Claims (Store Metadata / Promo Copy)
- "optimize recovery"
- "prevent overtraining"
- "maximize performance"
- "AI coach decides your training"
- "tell you exactly what to do today"

## Allowed Positioning
- "observes training patterns"
- "evidence-bound training insights"
- "bounded weekly interpretation"
- "coverage-aware interpretation"
- "makes uncertainty visible"

## Mandatory Disclosure
Store and onboarding copy must include:
- ZoneTruth does not diagnose or prescribe.
- Outputs are based on partial and evolving evidence coverage.

## Review Rule
All release metadata that mentions recovery, fatigue, performance, or recommendations must pass governance copy review before submission.
