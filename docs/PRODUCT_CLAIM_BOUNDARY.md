# Product Claim Boundary

## Product Position
ZoneTruth is an evidence-bound training observatory and decision-support surface.
It is not a medical, diagnostic, or prescriptive coaching product.

## ZoneTruth Does Not
- Diagnose medical or physiological conditions
- Prescribe training plans as authoritative intervention
- Provide medical guidance
- Guarantee recovery assessment accuracy
- Determine training suitability as a medical authority

## Output Contract
- Outputs are observational interpretations based on incomplete training signals.
- Inference must remain bounded by available evidence and disclosed coverage.
- Unknown/unavailable evidence is first-class output, not hidden implementation detail.

## Authority Levels
- Direct observation: measured and directly reported signals
- Bounded inference: weak-to-moderate synthesis over observed patterns
- Sparse inference: insufficient longitudinal evidence for strong claims

## Release Constraint
Any user-facing change that expands implied authority must be reviewed as a governance-risk change.
